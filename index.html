Breif Bio
I received the B.E. degree in Communication Engineering from the Tianjin University, Tianjin, China, in 2020. I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Ruiqin Xiong in Computer Science with Peking University, Beijing, China. My research interests include areas of image processing, computational photography and computer vision, particularly for topics related to motion estimation and neuromorphic cameras.
